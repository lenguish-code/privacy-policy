This Privacy Policy describes how \ ("Company," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, stores, shares, and protects your information when you use our language learning mobile application, LENGUISH (the "App").

1. CRITICAL DISCLOSURE REGARDING THIRD-PARTY LLM PROCESSING
The App's core functionality requires sending your text queries and commands (User Content) to third-party Large Language Model (LLM) providers (e.g., Google, ChatGPT, DeepL, OpenRouter, and future providers) for immediate processing (translation, sentence generation, etc.) [User Query].

WARNING: We cannot control or guarantee the security, retention policies, or use of your User Content by these independent third-party LLM providers. By submitting User Content, you acknowledge that the LLM provider will process this data according to their own privacy policies. You must not submit any sensitive, personally identifying, or confidential information in your queries, as this information is transmitted outside of our secure server environment.

2. DATA COLLECTION AND USE
We collect different types of data based on your interaction with the App, defined into the following categories:

2.1. Identity and Authentication Data
We collect data necessary to create and manage your account and provide essential services.

Data Collected: Email address, Display Name/User Name, and a unique user identifier provided by the authentication partner (e.g., Google ID, Apple ID).

Purpose: Account creation, secure sign-in, service provision (Contractual Necessity).

Source: Directly from you or via third-party authentication providers (e.g., Google Sign-In, Apple Sign-In).

2.2. User Content and LLM Processing Data
This data is essential for the language learning functionality and potential future synchronization features (AI chat, saved phrases).

Data Collected: Text queries (prompts) submitted to LLMs, the generated responses (translations, practice sentences), practice history, performance metrics, and saved learning materials.

Purpose: Providing the core service functionality, generating content, and future cross-device synchronization (Contractual Necessity).

2.3. Technical and Usage Data
Data collected automatically regarding your use of the App to monitor performance and improve services.

Data Collected: Device identifiers (non-advertising), operating system details, device configurations, IP address, interaction logs (features used, time spent, crash reports, error data).

Purpose: Service maintenance, analysis, optimization, and security (Legitimate Interest).

2.4. Advertising and Tracking Data
Data used to deliver, measure, and manage advertisements within the App.

Data Collected: Advertising IDs (e.g., AAID), data related to ad impressions and clicks, and non-precise location information (if provided by ad networks).

Purpose: Delivering contextual or personalized advertisements and measurement [User Query].

Note on Consent: Contextual advertising is part of the essential business model (Legitimate Interest). Personalized advertising requires explicit, separate user consent (Consent).

2.5. Transactional and Subscription Data
Data related to in-app purchases and subscriptions.

Data Collected: Subscription status (active/inactive), purchase history, transaction dates, and the type of subscription/bonus received.

Purpose: Fulfillment of subscription benefits and financial record-keeping (Contractual Necessity and Legal Obligation).

Disclaimer: We do not store or process sensitive payment information (credit card numbers, bank details). All payment processing is handled securely by the platform provider (e.g., Google Play).

Deletion Process: Upon 12 months of inactivity, we reserve the right to automatically and permanently delete the user's account, including all associated Identity Data and User Content stored on our server. We reserve the right not to send additional notice before final account deletion.

Data necessary to comply with legal, tax, accounting, and audit obligations, which may require retention for minimum statutory periods (e.g., records of subscription purchases).

Aggregated or de-identified usage data, which cannot be linked back to an individual, may be retained indefinitely for service improvement.
